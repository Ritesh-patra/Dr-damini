About Dr. Damini Verma

Consultant Dermatologist

Dr. Damini Verma is an experienced Consultant Dermatologist currently practicing as an Associate Consultant at Apollo Medics Hospital, Lucknow. She completed her MBBS from IMS, BHU and her MD in Dermatology from Lady Hardinge Medical College, New Delhi, followed by DNB in Dermatology.

With a strong foundation in clinical dermatology, pediatric dermatology, trichology, aesthetics, and dermatosurgery, Dr. Verma offers comprehensive care for skin, hair, nail, and sexually transmitted disorders. She is proficient in advanced procedures including lasers, PRP, microneedling, chemical peels, acne scar treatments, vitiligo surgeries, and aesthetic injectables.

An active academician and researcher, Dr. Verma has authored multiple national and international publications, serves as a reviewer for leading dermatology journals, and regularly participates as a speaker and panelist at CMEs and conferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get detailed answers to the most common queries our clients have.

Is Hydrafacial safe for sensitive skin?

Absolutely ✅ Hydrafacial is designed to be gentle and soothing for all skin types, including sensitive skin. The treatment can be easily customized based on your specific concerns, ensuring your skin feels clean, hydrated, and refreshed without irritation.

How many sessions are needed for acne scars?

The number of sessions depends on the type, age, and depth of your scars. Generally, 3–6 sessions are recommended for visible improvements. However, some individuals may see results even sooner, while others may require a few extra sessions for best outcomes.

Do you offer treatment packages?

Yes, we offer special value packs and flexible payment options so you can enjoy the best care at the best price. Whether you're looking for single treatments or complete skincare plans, we have packages tailored to suit your budget and goals.

What after-care should I follow post-treatment?

After your treatment, we’ll provide a personalized skincare routine to protect your results. This usually includes hydration tips, sun protection, and gentle cleansing. Avoid harsh products for a few days and keep your skin moisturized for the best glow ✨
